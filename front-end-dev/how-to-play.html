How to Play

Players must stay ahead of the beasts by answering a series of questions where each correct response allows our explorers to make one step towards safety. Sounds easy enough right?

Well try answering a series of multiple choice questions with a ten ton dinosaur chasing you down!

If our explorers answer too many incorrect questions then the dinosaurs will get to enjoy an early dinner.

Steps

1) Enter username and click 'Login' (or 'Register' if you don't have an account)

2) Answer questions correctly to ensure you stay alive. You only have 10 seconds per question!

3) Click 'Save your Score' at the end of the game.

Ready?
